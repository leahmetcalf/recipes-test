Delicious Spicy Pasta!

The best pasta you've ever eaten

This creamy tomato pasta is a vegan's dream! Impress even the most hesitant omnivores with with incredible recipe.

Ingredients

  • 500g of fusili pasta
  • 250g of chopped tomatoes
  • 200ml of vegan cream
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Fry the onion until transparent
  3. Add the garlic and cream and simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Pour the cooked pasta into the sauce and season to serve
